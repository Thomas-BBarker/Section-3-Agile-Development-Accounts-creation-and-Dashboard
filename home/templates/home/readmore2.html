Understanding and Managing Your Digital Identity

Your digital identity is split into two categories: your active trail (things you intentionally post, like social media updates and comments) and your passive trail (data collected behind the scenes, like your IP address, browser cookies, and search history). Cybercriminals and data brokers piece these fragments together to build profiling dossiers on you. To protect your identity, you must actively practice "data minimization"—sharing only what is absolutely necessary, using privacy-focused search engines, and routinely purging tracking data from your browser settings.
